Questions

Frequently Asked

What does the Day Pass include?

Your $15 Day Pass gives you full access to all PC gaming stations, the console lounge (PS5 + Nintendo), and the TV lounge for the entire day. No reservation needed — just walk in and play.

What's the difference between the three membership tiers?

Tier 1 – Community ($30/mo): 2 events/month, discounted hourly PC/console use, Member Discord access, and ability to reserve a station.

Tier 2 – Full Member ($70/mo): All weekly events, unlimited PC/TV use outside event times, priority tournament entry, 1 guest pass/month, and member perks (snacks, merch discounts, and more).

Tier 3 – VIP/Unlimited ($120/mo): Everything in Tier 2 plus unlimited PC/TV use anytime, free major tournament entry, Bring-a-Friend passes, exclusive merch or badge, and reserved seating at big events.

How do I sign up for a membership?

Stop by at 110 E University Ave Suite I, Urbana, IL, reach out via our contact page, call 217-418-7404, or join our Discord.

Can I book the space privately for a party or event?

Yes! We offer private bookings for birthdays, group events, and tournaments. Contact us to discuss availability and custom setups. Use the contact form or call 217-418-7404.

Are community events included with my membership?

Tier 1 members get access to 2 events per month. Tiers 2 and 3 include all weekly events, with VIP members also getting free entry to major tournaments and reserved seating. Check the events calendar and Discord for schedules.

What games are available?

A wide library across PC and consoles — competitive titles, fighting games, co-op games, and more. Join our Discord for the latest on available titles, or stop in and see for yourself.

Still Have Questions?
