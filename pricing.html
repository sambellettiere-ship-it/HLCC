Questions

Frequently Asked

What does the Day Pass include?

Your $15 Day Pass gives you unrestricted access to all PC gaming stations, the full console lounge (PS5 + Nintendo setups), and the TV lounge — for the entire day. No time limits per station during your session.

What are the perks of the Monthly Membership?

Members get unlimited daily visits, priority access to stations during busy times, and exclusive member perks. Members also get early or discounted access to special events. At $50/month it pays for itself in just a few visits.

How do I sign up for a membership?

Just stop by Hidden Level at 110 E University Ave Suite I, Urbana, IL, or reach out via our contact page, call us at 217-418-7404, or join our Discord.

Can I book the space privately for a party or event?

Yes! We offer private bookings for birthdays, group events, and tournaments. Contact us to discuss availability and what we can set up for you. Use the contact form or call 217-418-7404.

Are community events included with my pass or membership?

Yes — Better Together Night (every Friday) and Family Game Night (2nd Saturday monthly) are open to all visitors and members. Check our events calendar and Discord for any event-specific details or special activities.

What games are available?

We carry a wide library across PC and consoles including competitive titles, fighting games, co-op games, and casual fun. Join our Discord for the latest on available titles, or stop in and see for yourself.

Still Have Questions?

Reach out — we're happy to help. Or just come in and check it out.
