Questions

Frequently Asked

What does the Event Entry / Day Pass include?

Your $10 entry gives you two options: use the PC and console stations outside of any scheduled event, or attend one scheduled event (tournament, date night, LAN party, themed game night, etc.) and get full use of stations, prizes, and activities for that session. No membership required — just walk in and pay.

What does the Monthly Membership include?

The Monthly Membership ($50/mo) is your all-access pass to Hidden Level. It covers every scheduled public event that month — tournaments, date nights, LAN parties, themed game nights, and more — plus open access to the cafe during non-event hours. Excludes private event bookings.

How do I sign up for a membership?

Stop by at 110 E University Ave Suite I, Urbana, IL, reach out via our contact page, call 217-418-7404, or join our Discord.

How does private event booking work?

Private bookings are $150/hour and give your group exclusive use of the entire Hidden Level space — all PC stations, consoles, and the TV lounge — for up to 20 guests. We handle the setup and have staff on-site. Great for birthdays, team outings, graduation parties, and more. Contact us to check availability: use the contact form or call 217-418-7404.

Are community events included with my membership?

Monthly Members get unlimited entries to all scheduled public events that month. Check the events calendar and Discord for the full schedule.

What games are available?

A wide library across PC and consoles — competitive titles, fighting games, co-op games, and more. Join our Discord for the latest on available titles, or stop in and see for yourself.
