Who We Are

More Than a Café.
A Community Hub.

Hidden Level Cyber Cafe is Urbana-Champaign's gaming destination, located on University Ave in Urbana, IL. Whether you're a hardcore gamer or just looking for a fun night out, we have something for everyone.

Our café features PC gaming stations, a console lounge with PlayStation 5 and Nintendo setups, and a comfortable TV lounge — all backed by top-tier networking infrastructure for a seamless gaming experience.

Beyond the games, Hidden Level is a community hub. We host weekly community events like our Better Together Night, bringing locals together for shared gaming experiences, friendly competition, and good vibes.

We also offer private bookings, family game nights, and monthly memberships for regulars who want to make us their home base.
